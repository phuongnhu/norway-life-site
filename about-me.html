Thank you for visiting my blog!

I’m a 38-year-old woman living in Oslo. I’m not a KOL or influencer, I was simply enjoying my peaceful, average life without much thought about anything in particular.

Then, cancer suddenly entered my life. It made me pay more attention to many things and sparked a desire to do… something.

I created this blog primarily to document my journey with cancer (I would love to say “against cancer,” but in many cases, that might not be true). By sharing my experiences, I hope it can be helpful for some of you (or someone you know) who are going through the same thing.

Well, before, besides, and hopefully after cancer, I have a life with many little normal things that I’m happy to have chance to experience. That’s why this will not be a “pure cancer blog”, because I “try to live a normal life” as doctors advised me.

Wishing you a wonderful day, no matter the weather!
